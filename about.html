PROFESSOR GIRIRAJ NANDAN

Professor Giriraj Nandan was born on January 5, 1941 in Aonla, a town of Bareilly in Uttar Pradesh (India). His father, Ram Mukut Lal, died at when he was 3. He was then raised by his grandfather, Lala Ratan Lal, and mother Brahma Devi.

He was raised inculcating the feelings of patriotism and social service, which he acquired from his grandfather. He is a social worker, like his grandfather, a writer, a poet and a teacher.

He did Master of Arts twice in History and in Political Science. He taught in Subhash Inter College, Aonla, for 7 years as lecturer of History.

He has written several books on history and culture of Aonla, his birth place, Rohilkhand, and other towns and cities of Rohilkhand. He has published books on philosophy also. He has written editorials for various news-papers, which were based on philosophy and / or history.

He has been honored by various institutions. Most importantly he has been honored with the honors like Itihaas Ratn, Itihaas Vid, Yash-bharti, Bareilly-Gaurav.

He lives in the first house at Lala Ratan Lal Wali Gali, Aonla, with his wife and family.
