This is a portal giving information about the history of Rohilkhand, and its towns and cities like

• Aonla • Bareilly
• Badayun • Ahichchhatra
• Moradabad • Chandausi
• Sambhal • Paanchal
• Kampilya • Kather

through the books written by Professor Giriraj Nandan. He wrote books on places other than Ruhelkhand (Rohilkhand) also, like Lucknow.

These books contain detailed information of

• The history and culture of these places,
• Excavations done in these places,
• The emperors and kings who ruled here, and
• Public traditions

of these places.
